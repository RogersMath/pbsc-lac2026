Final Thought: Amplifying Human Value

AI used thoughtfully does not eliminate human value, it amplifies it. Imagine a field of grass as problems that need to be solved. Traditional work requires large groups hand coding and directly researching individual sources. It's like cutting grass with scissors. It can be done, but coordination is hard, and it takes an army of labor.

AI is like an industrial mower, where one person can plough down a large number of problems, but also might break things if they drive recklessly.

Some speak of a crisis of meaning. Well we've got a LOT of problems. I don't worry about running out of problems.

Reach out. Help me mow.
